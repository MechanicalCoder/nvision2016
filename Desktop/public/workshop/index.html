Speech Robo Workshop

​ηvision, IIT Hyderabad is back with its second workshop. This time, as the name reads, it is about controlling your robot with direct voice commands. Imagine, how wonderful would it be to give instructions and get your work done!!

ηvision, IIT Hyderabad, in association with i3indya Technologies gives you an opportunity to learn how to control your wireless robot with voice commands. Using MATLAB for speech processing and Bluetooth modules for wireless communication, this is a unique opportunity to explore the fast developing field of Robotics

In this two day (14 hour) workshop, the following topics will be covered :

1. Development of Autonomous Robot

2. PC Controlled Robot

3. Voice Controlled Bot from PC

4. Bluetooth/Mobile controlled Robot

5. Design an installer package(.exe file)

6. Voice Controlled Bot from android mobile
