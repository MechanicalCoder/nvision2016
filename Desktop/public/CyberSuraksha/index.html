Cyber Suraksha

National Level Event on Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security

30 January 2016


Highlights


  • India’s 1st National Level Event based on Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security to be held among 6,000 students
  • Combined initiative and efforts of ηvision 2016, IIT Hyderabad and i3indya Technologies
  • Total Prizes worth INR 1 Lakh to be won

  • Students from 1500 colleges of India would be participating in the Event including IIT’s and NIT’s
  • Cyber Suraksha website to get more than 2 Million hits by the time of final event at IIT Hyderabad
  • Cyber Suraksha to be held in 8 cities of India with a min. participation of 500 students everywhere

A very unique and new challenge in the field of Ethical Hacking to be held for the very 1st time in India by i3indya Technologies in association with ηvision 2016, IIT Hyderabad whose Prelims would be conducted across 8 cities of India and Finale at ηvision 2016


EVENT FLOW




Register

You first register at the Zonal Center


Learn

You then attend a workshop on ETHICAL HACKING & CYBER SECURITY


Prelims

You will then take part in the zonal prelim round which lasts for 45 mins.


Finale

5 students from each center will be shortlisted for a FINALE @ IIT Hyderabad

ηvision, 2016

The top students from each zone will compete in an epic finale at ηvision, 2016



Three Rounds


Round 1 (Workshop at Zonal Center):

  • To participate in Cyber Suraksha, students will firstly register at any Zonal Center across India
  • Attend 2 Days workshop (by i3indya Technologies) at the Zonal Centers
  • FREE Takeaway material + Certificate would be provided to participants
  • The duration of workshop will be of 12 Hours

Round 2 (Prelims Round at Zonal Center):

  • At the end of workshop the Prelims of Cyber Suraksha would be conducted (45 Mins) for all the participants who have attended the workshop
  • 5 TOP SHORTLISTED STUDENTS will be invited for the FINALE ROUND of "Cyber Suraksha" to be held at ηvision 2016, IIT Hyderabad.

Round 3 (At ηvision 2016, IIT Hyderabad):

  • Shortlisted Teams from all Zonal Centers will meet at the final round of Cyber Suraksha at ηvision'16, IIT Hyderabad on 29-31 January 2016.
  • Prizes worth Rs 1 Lakh to be won.

NOTE: The Prelims Round at Zonal Center will be conducted from 1st December 2015 to 28th January 2016


How to Participate:


Step 1: Register Online at www.nvision.org.in/CyberSuraksha (Coming Soon)

Step 2: Get a Unique ID at your email ID

Step 3: Show the Screenshot of confirmation mail and Unique ID at the Registration Desk of Zonal Center

Step 4: Attend workshop and get direct entry to the Prelims round on the 2nd day of workshop

Step 5: Five (5) Shortlisted candidated would be send to finale round of Cyber Suraksha at ηvision 2016, IIT Hyderabad

FAQ:


Q: Who can Participate in Cyber Suraksha ?
A: Any B.Tech/B.E. student from any college/year/branch having knowledge of Basic Internet can participate in the event

Q: Any Registration Fees for Cyber Suraksha ?
A: No registration fees at Zonal Center but will be for finalist at ηvision 2016, IIT Hyderabad

Q: Will I get Certificate of Participation after attending workshop at Zonal Center ?
A: Yes, from ηvision ’16, IIT Hyderabad and i3indya Technologies

Q: Is there any other way to participate in Cyber Suraksha ?
A: No, you have to firstly attend the workshop followed by the Problem Statement given at zonal center

Q: Can I register at all zonal centers?
A: No, A participant can register only at 1 zonal center and his registration would/can be cancelled at other zonal center
